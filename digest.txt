⑤ Aaron Levie · CEO @ Box

Levie 指出，企业实现 AI 自动化的核心瓶颈并非 agent 本身的能力，而是如何为其提供正确的上下文。相比编程场景（代码库天然提供结构化上下文），通用知识工作领域的这一挑战要复杂得多。

---

Levie highlighted that the core bottleneck for enterprise AI automation isn't the agents themselves, but getting the right context to them — a challenge that's much harder in general knowledge work than in coding, where the codebase naturally provides structured context.
