🎙 Podcast

Mistral: Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & Mistral 4 — w/ Pavan Kumar Reddy & Guillaume Lample

Mistral发布了Voxtral TTS——一个支持九种语言的30亿参数文本转语音模型，以远低于竞争对手的成本实现了业界领先的性能，底层基于其Ministral基础模型。最令人惊喜的是，该模型采用了一种全新的自研自回归流匹配架构，结合以12.5Hz潜在令牌运行的神经音频编解码器，摒弃了常见的离散深度Transformer方案，转而使用连续潜在表示以获得更高音质。该编解码器被独特地训练为同时支持离散和连续模式，通过流匹配头将带噪潜在向量去噪为80毫秒音频帧，而非自回归地预测离散令牌序列。首席科学家Guillaume Lample与音频研究负责人Pavan Kumar Reddy还探讨了Mistral更广泛的音频路线图，指出拥有数十年专有领域数据的企业客户在使用闭源模型时无法利用这些数据，而Mistral通过开放权重发布正是要弥补这一差距。

Mistral has released Voxtral TTS, a 3B text-to-speech model supporting nine languages that achieves state-of-the-art performance at a fraction of competitors' costs, built atop their Ministral base model. Most surprisingly, the model introduces a novel in-house autoregressive flow-matching architecture combined with a neural audio codec that operates at 12.5Hz latent tokens — eschewing the common discrete depth-transformer approach in favor of continuous latent representations that produce higher audio quality. The codec is uniquely trained to be both discrete and continuous, with a flow-matching head that denoises latents into 80ms audio frames rather than predicting discrete token sequences autoregressively. Chief Scientist Guillaume Lample and audio research lead Pavan Kumar Reddy also discussed Mistral's broader audio roadmap, noting that enterprise customers sitting on decades of proprietary domain data are significantly underserved by closed-source models that cannot access that data — a gap Mistral explicitly aims to fill with open-weight releases.
